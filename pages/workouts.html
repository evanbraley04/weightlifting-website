Overview of My Workouts

Throughout the week I do three different workouts. Together they are a variation on the popular Push, Pull, Legs split where you train chest, shoulders and triceps on a push day, back and biceps on a pull day, and then, as the name would suggest, legs on a leg day. Below you will find a list of all of the exercises I do on a given day. Generally, these exercises are done in 3 sets of 8-12 reps.
