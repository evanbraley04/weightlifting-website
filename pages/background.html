My Background

My whole life, I was very skinny. I ran cross-country and track, and didn't eat a whole lot. Before I started working out, I was 115 lbs at 5' 7" tall. Around April of 2021, one of my best friends who was training for football, asked me if I wanted to go to the gym with him. I thought it would be a fun way to maybe get healthier, but it ended up opening the door to one of the most exhilirating hobbies I have. From that day on, I became viciously motivated and hungry for weighlifting. I started lifting 6 days a week, monitoring my calories, and centering my days around the gym. It became an obsession. Over the course of a year I had gained over 30 lbs and looked completely different. Over the past year and a half, I have tried many different techniques, and learned many things about weightlifting and how to transform your body. My goal with this website is to inform you about how I did this, and how it changed my life for the better!
